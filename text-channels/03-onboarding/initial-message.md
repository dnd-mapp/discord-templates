# 🗺️ Welcome to the D&D Mapp Onboarding

Welcome, adventurer (and/or engineer)! You’ve joined the development and community hub for the **D&D Mapp** engine. To get started, please follow these steps to configure your "character sheet" for this server.

### 🛡️ Step 1: Read the Codex

Before you dive in, head over to [#rules-and-roles].

- Read our community guidelines.
- **Pick your Roles:** Are you a **Developer**, a **Dungeon Master**, or a **Player**? Selecting your roles will unlock specific areas of the server.

### 📢 Step 2: Stay Informed

Check [#announcements] for the latest platform updates and new feature releases.

### 💬 Step 3: Join the Conversation

- **General Interest:** Use [#general] for TTRPG talk, campaign stories, and general 5e chat.
- **Technical Deep-Dives:** If you want to talk **Angular, NestJS, Prisma,** or system architecture, head to [#dev-channel]. This is where we discuss the "engine" under the hood.

### 🎙️ Step 4: Voice Comms

- Feel free to jump into **General** (Voice) for live sessions or dev hangouts.

> **Note**  
> Our server is optimized for efficiency! If you are AFK for more than 5 minutes, you will be automatically moved to the **Inactive** channel to keep the lines clear.

### 🛠️ Step 5: Explore the Platform

If you haven't already, check out the live environment:

👉 https://dndmapp.nl.eu.org  
👉 https://github.com/dnd-mapp

**Need Help?**  
If you encounter any issues or have questions about the (A)RBAC permissions, feel free to tag a Moderator.

*Good luck, and may your rolls be ever in your favor!*
